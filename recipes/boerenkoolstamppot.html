Peel the potatoes and cut into equal pieces. Boil in water with salt for 20 minutes.
Drain. Let the potatoes boil dry in the saucepan on the smallest burner over low heat for 2 minutes. The less moisture in the potatoes, the fluffier the mash.
Heat the milk in a saucepan over low heat, but do not let it boil.
Mash the potatoes with the puree masher. Add the milk and butter and stir lightly with a whisk until the butter has completely melted. Season with the nutmeg, pepper and salt if desired.
EI-Gehaktbal
3 Eggs
300 g mixed grinded meat
40 g Verstegen gehakt lekkerste balletje
1 el mustard
3 grinded biscuits
60 g flower
In a bowl, mix the grinded meat with the spice mix, 1 raw egg and the mustard. Knead through and turn 2 balls.
Sprinkle the grinded biscuits in a deep plate. Whip 1 egg in another deep plate. Sprinkle the flour in a third plate and Peel the 2 boiled eggs.
Make the egg meatballs. Flatten a ball of grinded meat between damp hands. Place an egg on top and knead the minced meat neatly around the egg. Make the meatball egg-shaped. Make 2 meatballs like this.
Roll the egg meatballs first through the flour, then through the egg and finally through the breadcrumbs. Place them carefully in the hot oil and fry them on medium heat for 6-8 minutes until brown and done. Turn them regularly.
